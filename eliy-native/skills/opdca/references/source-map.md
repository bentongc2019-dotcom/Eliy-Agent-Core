- **Owner / Author** — Responsible party or original author.
- **Status** — `placeholder`, `draft`, `reviewed`, `accepted`, `deprecated`, `superseded`.
- **Reference Area** — One primary reference area from the ten defined areas.
- **Notes** — Free-text notes for future reference.
